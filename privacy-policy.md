# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 5, 2026*

Happy Pet Product Reviews ("we," "us," or "our") operates happypetproductreviews.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Information We Collect

**Automatically collected data:** When you visit our site, standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and pages visited. We use this data only in aggregate to understand site traffic.

**No account registration:** We do not require you to create an account or submit personal information to read our content.

## Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

- **Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data such as pages visited and time on site. You can opt out at [tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
- **Affiliate tracking:** We participate in affiliate programs including Amazon Associates, Chewy, and others. These programs may use cookies to track referrals and attribute purchases. We do not control these third-party cookies.

## Affiliate Disclosure

Happy Pet Product Reviews participates in affiliate programs. When you click a product link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence our editorial recommendations.

## Third-Party Services

Our site may interact with the following third parties, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **Amazon Associates** — [amazon.com/privacy](https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=GX7NJQ4ZB8MHFRNJ)

## Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data collected through third-party services (analytics, affiliate networks) is governed by those services' retention policies.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data held about you by third-party services we use. Please contact those services directly.

California residents may have additional rights under CCPA. We do not sell personal information.

## Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
